PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "MBIRA"
========================================

SYLLABLES: mbi - ra

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [mb] → [mb]
   Type: Prenasalized Stop
   Instruction: A very brief nasal airflow immediately precedes the 'b' stop within the same syllable.
   ⚠️  WARNING: The nasal sound is very short, almost part of the 'b'.

2. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.

3. [r] → [r]
   Type: Liquid
   Instruction: Standard English 'r' sound.

4. [a] → [a]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ah' as in 'father'. Short, pure, static sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'a' in 'cat' or 'make'. No gliding.
