PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "BHAZI"
========================================

SYLLABLES: bha - zi

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [bh] → [bʱ]
   Type: Breathy-Voiced Plosive
   Instruction: KEYWORD: AUDIBLE SIGH. An English 'b' sound with a simultaneous, audible release of air. The 'h' is a feature, not a separate sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce as 'b' followed by 'h' (e.g., 'ab-hor'). It is one single, airy sound.

2. [a] → [a]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ah' as in 'father'. Short, pure, static sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'a' in 'cat' or 'make'. No gliding.

3. [z] → [z]
   Type: Voiced Fricative
   Instruction: Standard English 'z' sound.

4. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.
