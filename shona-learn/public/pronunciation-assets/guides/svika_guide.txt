PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "SVIKA"
========================================

SYLLABLES: svi - ka

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [sv] → [swhistling]
   Type: Voiceless Whistled Sibilant
   Instruction: KEYWORD: WHISTLED SIBILANT. A unique high-frequency 's' sound. Visually, lips are slightly rounded/protruded.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT produce a recreational whistle. It is a sibilant friction sound. DO NOT pronounce as 's' + 'v'.

2. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.

3. [k] → [k]
   Type: Voiceless Stop
   Instruction: Standard English 'k' sound.

4. [a] → [a]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ah' as in 'father'. Short, pure, static sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'a' in 'cat' or 'make'. No gliding.
