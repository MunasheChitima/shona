PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "NGOMA"
========================================

SYLLABLES: ngo - ma

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [ng] → [ŋɡ]
   Type: Prenasalized Stop
   Instruction: A prenasalized hard 'g' consonant, not a full separate vowel sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'ng' in the English word 'sing'. It is 'ng' as in 'finger'.

2. [o] → [o]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'oh' as in 'so'. No 'w' glide.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT add a 'w' sound at the end as in the English 'go'.

3. [m] → [m]
   Type: Nasal
   Instruction: Standard English 'm' sound.

4. [a] → [a]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ah' as in 'father'. Short, pure, static sound.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'a' in 'cat' or 'make'. No gliding.
