PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "ZVINO"
========================================

SYLLABLES: zvi - no

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [zv] → [zwhistling]
   Type: Voiced Whistled Sibilant
   Instruction: KEYWORD: VOICED WHISTLED SIBILANT. Voiced version of sv. High-frequency 'z' with lip rounding.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce as 'z' + 'v'.

2. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.

3. [n] → [n]
   Type: Nasal
   Instruction: Standard English 'n' sound.

4. [o] → [o]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'oh' as in 'so'. No 'w' glide.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT add a 'w' sound at the end as in the English 'go'.
