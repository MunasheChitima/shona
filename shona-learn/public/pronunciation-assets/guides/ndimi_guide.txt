PHONETIC GUIDE FOR "NDIMI"
========================================

SYLLABLES: ndi - mi

PHONEME-BY-PHONEME BREAKDOWN:
1. [nd] → [nd]
   Type: Prenasalized Stop
   Instruction: A very brief nasal airflow immediately precedes the 'd' stop within the same syllable.
   ⚠️  WARNING: The nasal sound is very short, almost part of the 'd'.

2. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.

3. [m] → [m]
   Type: Nasal
   Instruction: Standard English 'm' sound.

4. [i] → [i]
   Type: Pure Vowel
   Instruction: Pronounce as 'ee' as in 'see'.
   ⚠️  WARNING: DO NOT pronounce like the 'i' in 'sit'.
