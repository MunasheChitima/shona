# Shona Language Learning App - Lesson Plans Implementation Guide

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive lesson plans for a Shona language learning application, following a scientifically-grounded pedagogical framework that combines Communicative Language Teaching (CLT), Task-Based Language Learning (TBLL), and strategic gamification.

## Pedagogical Framework

### Core Principles
1. **Communicative Language Teaching (CLT)** - Focus on real-world communication skills
2. **Task-Based Language Learning (TBLL)** - Learn through meaningful, practical tasks
3. **Cultural Integration** - Ubuntu/Unhu principles woven throughout
4. **Dual Pathways** - Age-appropriate content for "Sarura Kids" (5-12) and "Shona Pro" (13+)

### Scientific Foundation
- **Comprehensible Input** - Content at i+1 level for optimal acquisition
- **Spaced Repetition System** - For long-term vocabulary retention
- **Active Recall** - Effortful retrieval to strengthen memory
- **Cultural Authenticity** - Respect and proper cultural representation

## Curriculum Structure

### Level 1: Tsvarudzo (The Foundation) - CEFR A1/A2
**Duration:** 3 months | **Vocabulary Target:** 1,000-1,500 words

#### Units:
1. **Mhoro, Shamwari! (Hello, Friend!)** - Greetings and basic introductions
2. **Mhuri Yangu (My Family)** - Family vocabulary and possessive structures  
3. **Zvekudya (Food and Drink)** - Food vocabulary and expressing preferences
4. **Mazuva neNguva (Days and Time)** - Time expressions and daily schedules
5. **Nzvimbo (Places)** - Location vocabulary and directions
6. **Uwandu (Numbers and Counting)** - Numbers and basic mathematics

**Cultural Theme:** Ubuntu/Unhu basics - respect and community
**Grammar Focus:** Basic sentence structure, present tense, possessives, noun classes 1-2

### Level 2: Kukura (The Explorer) - CEFR B1
**Duration:** 4 months | **Vocabulary Target:** 3,000-5,000 words

#### Units (Sample):
1. **Zuva Rangu (My Day)** - Daily routines and time expressions
2. **Kutenda Musika (Going to Market)** - Market vocabulary and transactions
3. **Mamiriro Ekunze (Weather)** - Weather and seasons
4. **Zvido Zvangu (My Hobbies)** - Interests and activities
5. **Basa neRamepo (Work and Profession)** - Career and work vocabulary
6. **Mhuri Huru (Extended Family)** - Extended family and relationships

**Cultural Theme:** Deeper Ubuntu understanding - community responsibilities
**Grammar Focus:** Past tenses (recent vs remote), future tense, more noun classes

### Level 3: Kutaura (The Communicator) - CEFR B2
**Duration:** 5 months | **Vocabulary Target:** 5,000+ words

#### Units (Sample):
1. **Nhau Dzezuva (News of the Day)** - Current events and opinions
2. **Tsika Nemagariro (Culture and Traditions)** - Deep cultural exploration
3. **Ngano (Story Time)** - Traditional stories and narratives
4. **Matambudziko eZuva (Daily Challenges)** - Problem-solving language
5. **Kukura kweZimbabwe (Zimbabwe's Growth)** - History and development
6. **Dialect Spotlight** - Regional variations and dialects

**Cultural Theme:** Cultural mastery and regional awareness
**Grammar Focus:** Complex sentences, conditionals, subjunctive mood

### Level 4: Uchenjeri (The Orator) - CEFR C1
**Duration:** 6 months | **Vocabulary Target:** Advanced fluency

#### Units (Sample):
1. **Tsumo Nemadimikira (Proverbs and Idioms)** - Advanced expressions
2. **Kupenengura Zvinyorwa (Analyzing Texts)** - Literary analysis
3. **Hurukuro Yakadzama (In-depth Discussion)** - Complex conversations
4. **Nhau dzeZvematongerwo (Political Discussion)** - Advanced topics
5. **Nyaya dzeRudzi (Cultural Heritage)** - Deep cultural knowledge
6. **The Shonaverse (Capstone)** - Real-world application

**Cultural Theme:** Cultural leadership and advanced expression
**Grammar Focus:** Advanced structures, rhetoric, formal/informal registers

## Implementation Guidelines

### Exercise Types by Age Group

#### Sarura Kids (Ages 5-12)
- **Songs and Rhymes** - Traditional Shona children's songs
- **Interactive Stories** - Animated ngano (folktales)
- **Simple Games** - Matching, sorting, tap-to-hear activities
- **Visual Learning** - Colorful characters like "Gogo Alice"
- **Play-Based Learning** - Learning through game mechanics

#### Shona Pro (Ages 13+)
- **Structured Lessons** - Clear grammar explanations
- **Authentic Materials** - News articles, podcasts, literature
- **Spaced Repetition** - Digital flashcard systems

## Cultural Integration Strategy

1. **Respect in Communication** - Formal vs informal address
2. **Community Responsibility** - Family and social obligations
3. **Intergenerational Wisdom** - Learning from elders
4. **Collective Decision Making** - Consensus-building language
5. **Hospitality and Welcome** - Guest reception protocols

- Traditional ceremonies and celebrations
- Family gatherings and meal sharing
